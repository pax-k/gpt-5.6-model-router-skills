# Policy attestations

For the v0.3.0 skills-only candidate:

- Listing and starter prompts describe the implemented routing-only behavior.
- The plugin has no authentication, MCP server, connector, hosted service, analytics, advertising, tracking, or credential collection.
- Local setup writes are limited to ten managed templates, backups, one managed depth entry, and its ownership state. It preserves unrelated configuration edits.
- The plugin does not bypass runtime permissions. Model, effort, review, fork, and fallback choices remain advisory root decisions.
- Routed children are leaves unless given the exact one-level grant; every descendant is a leaf. Compact path-based briefs are the default.
- External, destructive, credentialed, costly, and out-of-scope actions remain subject to active Codex authority.
- The candidate contains no secrets, private rollout identifiers, local absolute paths, cached bytecode, or private acceptance artifacts.
